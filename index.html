Game Development by Caspar Dunant (SpadXIII)

Always dreamed of making games since I was 12 years old. In 2016 I finally started making games in my spare time.

Irenic

Another project I lead at Gamkedo.club. A Mahjong connect game with four different game modes and four different tile-sets.

Play in-browser

Journey of Little 2-bit

The first project I lead at Gamkedo.club. A shoot-em-up about little 2-bit. A space ship tasked with delivering a package. Along the way little 2-bit encounters a range of enemies and gets to use several different weapons.

Play in-browser

Critical Alpha - Afterburner

A game initially led by Tim Waskett and later by Nicholas Polchies at the Gamkedo.club. I made the model of a Spad-XIII and a Fokker Dr.I, which were used in the 1918 levels for the player and the enemy airplanes.

APC5

A game let by Nicholas Polchies at the Gamkedo.club. I helped with coding the particle effects, music/sound toggle buttons, and the cannon ball power-up.
